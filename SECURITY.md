# Security Policy

This repository holds community documentation, guides, and media — not production application code. SoterCare's product code lives in the organization's [other repositories](https://github.com/SoterCare).

## Reporting a vulnerability

If you find a security or privacy issue in **this** repository — for example, an exposed credential accidentally committed, a malicious link, or a photo that shouldn't be public — please report it privately rather than opening a public issue:

- Use GitHub's [**Report a vulnerability**](https://github.com/SoterCare/community/security/advisories/new) (Security → Advisories) for anything sensitive
- Or reach the core team via [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/company/sotercare)

Please include what you found, where, and (if relevant) how to reproduce it. We'll acknowledge your report, investigate, and act promptly.

## Reporting issues in SoterCare products

For vulnerabilities in a specific SoterCare application, use the security policy of that project's repository. If none exists, contact the core team and we'll route it.

## Our commitment

- We respond to good-faith reports quickly and treat them confidentially.
- We credit reporters who wish to be named.
- We never take legal action against researchers acting in good faith.

Thank you for helping keep our community and its members safe.
