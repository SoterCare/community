# ⛓️ Blockchain / Web3

SoterCare has run workshops on both Algorand and Solana. These resources get you building on-chain.

## Algorand

- [Algorand Developer Portal](https://developer.algorand.org/) — official docs, tutorials, and the AlgoKit tooling from our [workshop partner](../partners/algorand.md)
- [AlgoKit](https://developer.algorand.org/docs/get-started/algokit/) — the toolkit we used in the [Algorand Foundation Workshop](../events/algorand-workshop.md)

## Solana

- [Solana Developer Resources](https://solana.com/developers) — official starting point for Solana development
- [Solana Cookbook](https://solana.com/developers/cookbook) — practical, recipe-style guides

## Fundamentals

- [Ethereum.org — Learn](https://ethereum.org/en/learn/) — clear, vendor-neutral explanations of core blockchain concepts
- [CryptoZombies](https://cryptozombies.io/) — learn smart-contract development by building a game

## Why it matters

Web3 introduces new models for trust, ownership, and coordination — and it's a fast-growing space where student builders can stand out early.
