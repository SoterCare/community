# 📘 Community Handbook

The one-page overview of how the SoterCare community works. New here? Read this, then follow the links that apply to you.

## Who we are

SoterCare is a student-led developer community building open-source healthcare technology across AI, Cloud, IoT, Blockchain, and software engineering. We don't just build products — we build developers. See the [main README](../README.md) for the full picture.

## How the community is structured

Everyone starts as a **Member** and grows through contribution:

```
Member → Contributor → Volunteer / Mentor → Organizer → Maintainer → Core Team
```

The full model, decision-making, and handover process are in [Governance](../GOVERNANCE.md).

## Our values

1. Beginners are welcome — always.
2. We learn in public.
3. We ship real things.
4. We credit generously.
5. We build for health, and care for our community.

## How we communicate

- **GitHub Issues** — concrete, actionable tasks
- **GitHub Discussions** — questions, ideas, introductions, and announcements
- **Social media** — [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/company/sotercare), [Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/sotercare_/), [YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/@SoterCare)

## Expectations

Everyone follows the [Code of Conduct](../CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md) in every community space. Be kind, be constructive, and help the person behind you.

Welcome to SoterCare. We're glad you're here. 💙
