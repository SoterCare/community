# 💬 Discussion Categories

How [GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/SoterCare/community/discussions) is organized. Issues are for concrete, actionable tasks; Discussions are for everything conversational.

| Category | Format | Use it for |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 📣 **Announcements** | Announcement (maintainers post) | Events, programs, important community news |
| 👋 **Introductions** | Open discussion | Introduce yourself — who you are, what you want to learn |
| 💡 **Ideas** | Open discussion | Early-stage ideas not ready to be an issue yet |
| 🙏 **Q&A** | Question/Answer | Help with contributions, Git, our projects, career questions |
| 🎪 **Events** | Open discussion | Pre-event coordination, post-event conversation, follow-up questions for speakers |
| 🌟 **Show and Tell** | Open discussion | Share what you built, your first PR, your wins |
| 🗳️ **Polls** | Poll | Community input on decisions (topics, timings, names) |

## Conventions

- Introduce yourself in **Introductions** before your first post elsewhere — it helps us welcome you properly
- Mark accepted answers in **Q&A** so future readers find solutions fast
- An idea that gains traction in **Ideas** graduates to an issue with the `proposal` label
- The [Code of Conduct](../CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md) applies everywhere, Discussions included

> **Maintainer setup note:** categories are configured in **Settings → Discussions** to match this table.
