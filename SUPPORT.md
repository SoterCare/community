# Support

Need help? Here's how to find it in the SoterCare community.

## I have a question

- 💬 **[GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/SoterCare/community/discussions)** — the best place for questions about contributing, our projects, events, or getting started. Use the **Q&A** category.
- 📖 **[Community FAQ](FAQ.md)** — answers to the most common questions.

## I'm new and want to contribute

Start with the [First Contribution Guide](guides/first-contribution.md) and look for [`good first issue`](https://github.com/SoterCare/community/issues?q=is%3Aissue+is%3Aopen+label%3A%22good+first+issue%22) labels. A [mentor](guides/become-a-mentor.md) can help if you get stuck.

## I found a problem in this repository

Open a [Bug Report](https://github.com/SoterCare/community/issues/new/choose). For anything security- or privacy-related, follow the [Security Policy](SECURITY.md) instead.

## I want to propose an event, workshop, or idea

Open the matching template from [New Issue](https://github.com/SoterCare/community/issues/new/choose):
Event Proposal · Workshop Proposal · Community Suggestion.

## I want to reach SoterCare directly

- 🌐 [sotercare.com](https://sotercare.com)
- 💼 [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/company/sotercare) · 📸 [Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/sotercare_/) · ▶️ [YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/@SoterCare)

## What we don't support here

This repo is the community hub. For help with a specific SoterCare product, use that project's own repository and issues.
