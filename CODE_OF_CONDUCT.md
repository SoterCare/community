# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

SoterCare is committed to an open, inclusive, and welcoming community. We pledge to make participation a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

**Behavior that builds our community:**

- Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Welcoming beginners and encouraging learning at every level
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Taking responsibility for our mistakes and learning from them
- Focusing on what is best for the community as a whole

**Behavior that is not acceptable:**

- Harassment, discrimination, or abusive language of any kind
- Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, personal or political attacks
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission
- Sexualized language or imagery, or unwelcome advances
- Any conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all SoterCare spaces — GitHub repositories, issues, pull requests, discussions, events (online and in person), workshops, and social media — and whenever someone represents the community publicly.

## Enforcement

Violations can be reported to the community organizers through [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/company/sotercare) or our [website](https://sotercare.com). All reports will be reviewed promptly and treated confidentially. Organizers may take any action they deem appropriate, from a warning to permanent exclusion from community spaces.
