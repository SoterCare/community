# 🏛️ Community Governance

How the SoterCare community is organized and how decisions get made.

## Roles

| Role | Who they are | How you get there |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Member** | Anyone who participates — attends events, joins discussions | Show up! No application needed |
| **Contributor** | Anyone with a merged PR or organized activity | Make your first contribution |
| **Volunteer** | Members who help run events and programs | See the [Volunteer Guide](guides/volunteer-guide.md) |
| **Mentor** | Experienced members guiding newcomers | See [Become a Mentor](guides/become-a-mentor.md) |
| **Organizer** | Volunteers who lead events, workshops, or programs | Nominated by organizers after consistent volunteering |
| **Maintainer** | Organizers with repository write access | Invited by the core team after sustained contribution |
| **Core Team** | Community leads setting direction | Elected annually by organizers and maintainers |

## Decision Making

- **Day-to-day decisions** (merging PRs, event logistics) — made by maintainers and organizers directly
- **Community decisions** (new programs, partnerships, roadmap changes) — proposed as a GitHub Discussion or issue, open for community input for at least one week, decided by the core team with the feedback in view
- **Governance changes** (this document, Code of Conduct) — require core team consensus and a public announcement

We optimize for **doing things** over process. When in doubt, open an issue and ask.

## Transparency

- Plans live in the public [Roadmap](ROADMAP.md)
- Event outcomes are documented in [`events/`](events/), [`workshops/`](workshops/), and [`hackathons/`](hackathons/)
- Recognition is public — see [Contributor Recognition](docs/recognition.md)

## Term & Handover

Because we're student-led, leadership naturally rotates. Core team members serve one academic year, and every organizer role has a written handover doc (see [`templates/`](templates/)) so knowledge outlives any single batch of students.

## Conflict Resolution

Disagreements are resolved in the open where possible (issues/discussions). Interpersonal or Code of Conduct matters go privately to the core team — see [enforcement](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md#enforcement).
