# 🏅 CuttingEdge 2026 PROJEXPO IIT

> SoterCare at CuttingEdge 2026 PROJEXPO — **Best SDGP Community & Project**, and **2nd Runner-Up** overall.

**Date:** July 2026 · **Focus:** Innovation / Project Expo · **Venue:** Informatics Institute of Technology (IIT)

## Overview

SoterCare showcased its Software Development Group Project (SDGP) at **CuttingEdge 2026 PROJEXPO**, IIT's project exhibition, earning recognition as the **Best SDGP Community & Project** and placing **2nd Runner-Up**.

## Objectives

- Present SoterCare's SDGP to judges, industry, and peers
- Demonstrate both a strong product and a strong community around it
- Compete among the best student projects

## Our Role

Team SoterCare exhibited and presented their project throughout the expo.

## Event Highlights

- 🏅 Best SDGP Community & Project
- 🥈 2nd Runner-Up
- Live project demonstration at PROJEXPO

## Community Impact

- Recognized not just for the product but for the **community** built around it
- Validated SoterCare's dual focus: building technology *and* building developers

## Technologies

`Healthcare Technology` · `AI` · `IoT` · `Cloud` · `Full-Stack`

## Key Learnings

- Judges reward a project that has a living community behind it, not just code
- A polished demo plus a clear impact story wins expos
