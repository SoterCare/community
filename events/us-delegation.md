# 🌍 U.S. Delegation Visit — University of Oklahoma

> SoterCare featured during the University of Oklahoma delegation visit — international exposure for the team and its healthcare technology.

**Date:** January 2026 · **Focus:** Innovation / International Exposure · **Context:** SDGP

## Overview

SoterCare presented its work during a **U.S. Delegation Visit involving the University of Oklahoma**, showcasing the team's healthcare technology — including SoterCare Studio — to an international audience.

## Objectives

- Present SoterCare's healthcare technology to an international delegation
- Represent student innovation from our institution on a global stage
- Build connections beyond the local ecosystem

## Our Role

The SoterCare team presented and demonstrated their project directly to the visiting delegation.

## Event Highlights

- Live demonstration of SoterCare Studio (sensor data collection and visualization)
- Engagement with visiting U.S. delegation members
- International visibility for a student-built healthcare project

## Community Impact

- International exposure for SoterCare and its members
- Validation of the team's healthcare-technology direction

## Technologies

`Healthcare Technology` · `IoT` · `SoterCare Studio` · `Sensor Data`
