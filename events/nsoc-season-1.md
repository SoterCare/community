# 🤝 Nexus Spring of Code (NSoC)

> SoterCare as a **Community Partner** of Nexus Spring of Code — collaboration and Season 1 recognition.

**Date:** May – June 2026 · **Focus:** Open Source · **Program:** Nexus Spring of Code (NSoC)

## Overview

SoterCare partnered with **Nexus Spring of Code (NSoC)** as a **Community Partner**, collaborating on the open-source program and earning recognition at the close of **Season 1**.

## Objectives

- Support NSoC's open-source program and its participants
- Bring SoterCare's community into a wider open-source initiative
- Recognize and encourage student contributors

## Our Role

Community Partner — supporting the program, its promotion, and its contributors through Season 1.

## Event Highlights

- Community Partnership announced with NSoC
- Recognition at the end of NSoC Season 1

## Community Impact

- Extended SoterCare's open-source network
- Connected members to another active contributor community

## Technologies

`Open Source` · `Git` · `GitHub` · `Community Collaboration`

## Key Learnings

- Partnering with established programs grows both communities faster than going solo
