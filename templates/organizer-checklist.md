# 🧑‍✈️ Organizer Checklist

For the event lead — the person accountable for making it happen. Pair this with the [Event Checklist](event-checklist.md), which covers the full timeline; this one covers *your* responsibilities as lead.

## Ownership

- [ ] You have an approved proposal issue and it's assigned to you
- [ ] Every task in the [Event Checklist](event-checklist.md) has an owner (a name, not "the team")
- [ ] A co-lead can step in if you're unavailable

## Team

- [ ] Volunteers briefed on their roles and the [Code of Conduct](../CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md)
- [ ] Volunteers know who to escalate problems to (you)
- [ ] New volunteers paired with experienced ones — events are how we grow organizers

## Risk & safety

- [ ] Venue emergency info known (exits, contacts) for in-person events
- [ ] A plan for the three classic failures: speaker cancels, internet dies, half the registrants don't show
- [ ] Code of Conduct contact person named in opening remarks

## Communication

- [ ] Single source of truth for the plan (the planning issue), kept current
- [ ] Partners/sponsors know exactly what they're getting and when
- [ ] Announcement, reminder, and recap posts scheduled — not improvised

## Handover

- [ ] [Event Report](event-report.md) completed within a week, including honest lessons
- [ ] Everything a future organizer needs is in the repo, not in your DMs
- [ ] Volunteers and speakers thanked publicly, and recognized in [Contributor Recognition](../docs/recognition.md)
