# 🎤 Speaker Checklist

For anyone speaking at a SoterCare event — including first-time speakers. We *want* first-time speakers; this checklist exists so you feel prepared, not policed.

## When you're confirmed

- [ ] Send your [Speaker Information](speaker-information.md) to the organizers
- [ ] Read the [Code of Conduct](../CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md)
- [ ] Confirm session length and audience level with the organizers

## Preparing (1–2 weeks out)

- [ ] Slides drafted — aim for fewer, clearer slides over more, denser ones
- [ ] Hands-on parts tested end-to-end on a clean machine/account
- [ ] Prerequisites for attendees sent to organizers to share ahead of time
- [ ] Timed a full run-through at least once (leave room for Q&A)
- [ ] Backup plan ready: offline copies of slides/demos, screenshots of live demos

## Day before

- [ ] Tech check with organizers (display, adapters, internet, mic)
- [ ] Slides exported to PDF and sent to organizers

## On the day

- [ ] Arrive 30 minutes early (or join the call 15 minutes early)
- [ ] Repeat audience questions before answering (helps the room and any recording)
- [ ] Share where attendees can find you and the materials afterwards

## After

- [ ] Send final slides for [`slides/`](../slides/) — PDF, named `YYYY-MM-DD-topic-yourname.pdf`
- [ ] Answer follow-up questions in the event's discussion thread
- [ ] Optional but appreciated: a short reflection for the event report
